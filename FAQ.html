What is Gluten?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, but because of its close proximity when being farmed it can also be found in oat, barley, and rye. A lot of times these are also manufactured in the same processing plants.

What can you expect when you come into our restaurant?

A member of our compassionate, courteous staff will greet you at the front door with a smile.
